Traditional Bacalhau à Brás

Description

Traditional Bacalhau à Brás, portuguese recipe

In Portugal, there are 1001 ways to cook codfish, some more traditional and some more creative. The Portuguese love and consume cod anywhere in the country.

According to Dona Dolores, Bacalhau à Brás is one of the favorite dishes of her son and the best player in the world, Cristiano Ronaldo.

Ingredients

  • 400 g cod already soaked
  • 500 g bagged potato chips
  • 2 large onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 un eggs
  • q.s. salt
  • q.s. White pepper
  • q.s. Chopped parsley

Steps

  1. If you have chosen to use frozen cod, you only need to let it defrost and it is ready to use. If you prefer to use salted cod, you should let it soak for at least 20 to 24 hours in plenty of water.
  2. Blanch the cod for about 10 minutes in a pan of boiling water. Remove from the water and let it cool a little before removing the skins and bones. Now you can shred it into chips.
  3. Sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil, add the cod and let the oil absorb a little for 2 to 3 minutes
  4. Then add the straw potato involving well.
  5. When hot, add the previously beaten eggs seasoned with salt and pepper. Pay attention because if you use packet potatoes, you don't even need to add salt.
  6. Keep the heat low and mix well, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until the eggs reach a consistency similar to that of scrambled eggs.
