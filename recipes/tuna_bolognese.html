Tuna bolognese

Description

Tuna bolognese, portuguese recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cans of natural tuna
  • 400 g of spaghetti
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large tin of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 dl of olive oil
  • 1.5 dl of white wine
  • Chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna can. Peel the onion and garlic, chop everything very finely and place in a saucepan. Add the oil, bring boil and cook until the onion is soft.
  2. Add the chopped peeled tomatoes with the sauce and white wine, season with salt and pepper and bring to boil. Then add the tuna, mix gently and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook the spaghetti in water seasoned with salt for 9 minutes. Then drain it, pour it into a bowl or deep plate, cover with the tuna mixture and serve sprinkled with chopped parsley. If you like, accompany with grated cheese. Separate the dose to take to work and heat in the microwave.
